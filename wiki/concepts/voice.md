# Voice

Standalone components for building voice AI applications.

  - [Speech-to-Text](speech-to-text.md) — Real-time and file-based transcription with four engine options: Telnyx, Google, Deepgram, and Azure.

  - [Text-to-Speech](text-to-speech-overview.md) — Convert text to natural-sounding audio via WebSocket streaming. Multiple voice providers and languages.

  - [Voice Clone](../runbooks/voice-cloning.md) — Clone voices from audio samples or design custom AI voices from natural language prompts.
