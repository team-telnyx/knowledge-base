# Wireless

Programmable cellular connectivity — data, voice, and messaging for any device.

Telnyx Wireless gives you API control over cellular connectivity. Provision SIMs, route data through private networks, enable VoLTE calling, and send SMS — all programmable, all on one platform.

  - [SIMs & eSIMs](../runbooks/sim-cards.md) — Order, activate, and manage SIM cards. Group operations, bulk provisioning, and eSIM profiles.

  - [Data](../runbooks/sims-esims.md) — Cellular data with flexible routing — public internet, Private Wireless Gateways, or public IPs for inbound connections.

  - [VoLTE](https://developers.telnyx.com/docs/iot-sim/voice-enabled-iot) — A second line on one phone, or the full Telnyx voice stack on cellular — Call Control, AI agents, recording, IVR.

  - [Messaging](https://developers.telnyx.com/docs/iot-sim/messaging-settings) — SMS, MMS, and RCS on cellular numbers. A real messaging experience — same as any carrier line.
