# Speech-to-Text

Real-time and batch audio transcription via WebSocket, REST, or in-call.

Telnyx STT transcribes audio to text in three ways:

  - [WebSocket Streaming](../runbooks/lifecycle.md) — Stream audio over a persistent WebSocket connection. Real-time partial and final transcripts.

  - [File-Based Transcription](../runbooks/overview-2.md) — Upload audio files via REST API. OpenAI-compatible endpoint.

  - [In-Call Transcription](../runbooks/in-call-transcription.md) — Enable transcription during live voice calls via Call Control or TeXML.
