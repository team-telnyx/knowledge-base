# Testing

Test your edge functions locally and in staging before deploying to production.

  **Coming Soon** — Testing tooling and staging environments are in development.

Testing support will let you test your edge functions at every level — unit, integration, and end-to-end.

Since Edge Compute runs real containers, standard testing tools (pytest, Jest, go test, JUnit) work out of the box.
